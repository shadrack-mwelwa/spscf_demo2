Treatment

Shane Perry Smith Christian Foundation is identifying children with cerebral palsy from all socio-economic backgrounds irrespective of cultural, financial and geographical barriers. The Foundation guides the parents of such children to take their children for proper treatment using latest treatment modalities available. We have identified Stem Cell therapy to be the best form of intervention so far.

Stem Cell Therapy

In Stem cell therapy a small needle is injected into the hip to take out bone marrow. This is done under local anaesthesia.

Then the specimen is taken to a laboratory, where the bone marrow is separated from the Stem Cells. The stem cells are then taken and injected back into the patient's body. Where they will now go to the damaged areas of the body and accumulate, regenerate, and the body will expel the dead Stem Cells.

Therefore we only see a change after 3 months of treatment, however the patient has to undergo extensive rehabilitation after the stem cell therapy treatment. Then results are checked every 6 months after treatment.

We have parents in Zambia who have taken their children for Stem Cell therapy treatment and have seen improvement on their child. It's not a miracle over-night cure, but it does help the child and there is improvement.

OUR VISION FOR LOCALISED TREATEMENT

In order to provide various kinds of therapy services at one place Shane Perry Smith Christian Foundation plans to run a comprehensive therapy centre for children with these disabilities.

The centre will have comprehensive therapy facilities for various therapy services required by the child for attaining his/her full potential in everyday life activities. The centre will have experienced therapists specialized in pediatric therapy services. Almost 50-60 children will take advantage of our comprehensive therapy services on a daily basis and the number is likely to increase in the near future.

We shall a have multidisciplinary approach to address diverse needs of children with disability. The centre will provide the following types of therapy services:

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Pediatric Physiotherapy treatment aims to promote a child's independence and ability to reach physical milestones.

The Pediatric physiotherapists are specialists in child development and have specialist skills in evaluating, identifying, analyzing and treating child growth and movement issues.

The Pediatric physiotherapy program is specific to a child's need, age and abilities. The Physiotherapy treatment helps in mobilizing stiff joints, prevent deformities, improving functional strength, balance, posture and coordination in day to day activities.

Paediatric Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapy is a treatment that focuses on helping children to perform age-appropriate tasks or activities of daily living in the safest and most efficient manner possible.

The Occupational therapists evaluate child's skills for play activities, school performance, and activities of daily living and compare them to what is developmentally appropriate. The Occupational Therapy facility will provide quality services for children with physical, developmental, cognitive, and psychosocial disabilities.

The Occupational therapist will evaluate a child and plan therapy activities to minimize the effects of disease, injury, congenital deficit, disability, or developmental delay.

They train children for activities of daily living including: feeding, dressing, schooling activities etc. We will use play therapy to improve Childs engagement and participation in day to day activities.

Language, Speech and Communication Therapy

The Speech and Language Therapists will provide assessment and treatment for children in the areas of Augmentative and Alternative Communication, Dysphagia, Cognitive and Linguistic Development, Speech Articulation, Social Skills, Voice and Fluency/Stuttering.

The goal of speech and language intervention is to maximize the child's functional communication abilities across all settings.

Parents Training and Home Therapy Programs

Many children need repetitive therapy sessions on daily basis hence parents are trained for home therapy programs.

Also many parents can't afford to stay in city for therapy program of longer duration therefore they are trained for home therapy program by our therapist.

Home therapy programs and parents training ensure sustenance of benefits achieved by surgery and therapy.

Periodic Therapy Assessment

Children are periodically assessed to measure the impact of therapy intervention and designing future therapy strategies.

Prescribing Orthotic Appliances, Assistive Devices and Adaptive Equipment

Customized orthotic appliances are prescribed to maintain the corrections derived from therapy program.

Assistive devices are prescribed to enhance functional capabilities of the child. Commonly used orthotic appliances and assistive devices include- Ankle Foot Orthosis, Knee splints, CP chairs, Adaptive walkers etc are prescribed by our therapists.

We have so far identified 135 children that need to be taken for specialised treatment in India. Help us brighten the futures of these children.
