The Shane Perry Smith Christian Foundation

Shane Perry Smith is a Limited by Guarantee non-profit organization committed to providing medical care and rehabilitation services to children with Cerebral Palsy, Autism, Down Syndrome, Attention Deficit / Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) and other disabilities with interventions such as Physiotherapy, Speech Therapy, ABM (Anat Baniel Method) Therapy and Stem Cell Therapy.

Our Programs

Treatment Center

In order to provide various kinds of therapy services at one place Shane Perry Smith Christian Foundation plans to run a comprehensive therapy centre for children with these disabilities. The centre will have comprehensive therapy facilities for various therapy services required by the child for attaining his/her full potential in everyday life activities.

Joshua's Recovery Story

Joshua was the first child with Cerebral Palsy to be brought to us. He has made many strides on his journey to recovery.

Distributing Wheelchairs

On April 11 2022. We gave 20 wheelchairs to children with cerebral palsy in chongwe. It really was special because people in the village suffer financially. so to receive a special needs wheelchair was an enormous help to the child.

Successful Fundraising for Natashas Treatment

On November 29 2022. After hosting several Fundraising events. We managed to raise enough money for Natasha's airfares.

Our Vision

To enhance the capabilities of Children with Cerebral Palsy, Autism etc to live a dignified, independent and fulfilling life to his/her utmost potential without being discriminated or assumed a burden by the society.
